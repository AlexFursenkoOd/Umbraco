Translation Manager

Translation Manager helps you manage translations of your content between your sites within Umbraco.

Setup not Complete

You need to create a translation set, so that Umbraco knows where to send translated text to within the CMS.

Translation Sets are located in the settings section.

Create Translation Set

Pending

Items that have been updated in the master site, which will need to be translated to the languages in the other sites.

View

Submitted

Translation jobs that have been submitted for translation. Jobs will wait here until a translation is received back from the translation service.

View

Received

Things that have come back from the translators. From here you can approve and publish the translations onto the relevant sites in Umbraco.

Archived

All your past translation jobs.
